The African Lion

Unlike other cats, lions are very social animals. They live in groups, called prides, of around 30 lions.

  • Habitat: South Sahara desert and in parts of southern and eastern Africa.
  • Population:Fewer than 21,000 remain in all of Africa
  • Mating Season: Throughout the year
  • Gestation: 110 days

The Lions are the undisputed Kings of the Jungle, they have a mane and a loud Roar
