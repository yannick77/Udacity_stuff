Didier Drogba

Didier Drogba

Beastly Striker. Scorer of the infamous winning penalty of Chelsea's only Champions league Trophy. King Of Wembley.

Frank Lampard

Midfield Maestro. Captain on the night for both Chelsea's Champions League(0'13) and Europa League (0'12) triumphs and the best midfielder in history of EPL. Big game player.

Petr Cech

Imposing Goalkeeper. Best Player on that night in Munich, Saved a penalty in extra-time to keep Chelsea in the game then saved two more in the penalty shoot-out. Most clean sheets in EPL history. Safe Hands.

John Terry

Die Hard Defender. The most successful captain in EPL history, a local hero who was ready to risk it all for his club. Highest scoring defender in EPL History. Captain Leader Legend.
